Katagami · design-language bake-off

One concept, three models, judged blind.

Each model researched the concept — stargazing — with tools on, then created a design language and embodied it the Katagami way: a DESIGN.md + an embodiment, a landing, and a dashboard. The score is a five-part rubric — sourcing, synthesis, coherence, craft, distinctiveness — graded by a blind cross-model panel where no model scores its own work. The taste vote is yours.
