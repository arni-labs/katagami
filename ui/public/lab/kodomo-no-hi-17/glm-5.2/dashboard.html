02 Guide

Four things to do today.

1

Find your pole

Check your family number at the riverside gate, then walk the north bank to your pole. Carp are pre-tied; you only raise the line.

2

Write a wish carp

One paper carp per child, free at the children's tent. Write one wish — the simpler the better — and tie it where the wind is steadiest.

3

Eat the leaves

Kashiwa-mochi first (oak leaf, sweet bean), then chimaki (bamboo leaf, savoury). Both are meant to be eaten outside, on a mat.

4

Take the iris bath

The bath house opens at 16:00. Soak for the wish of health through summer. The leaves are gathered from the park that morning.
