Essentials

What to know before you arrive.

01

Entry

Free for all ages. No tickets required. Donations welcome at the welcome tent.

02

Getting here

Satsuki Park, 12-minute walk from Midori Station. Bike parking at the east gate.

03

Weather

The festival runs rain or shine. Covered areas are marked on the map.

04

Accessibility

Paved paths, accessible toilets, and reserved seating at every stage.

Satsuki Park
