Route notes

Move with the streamers.

Enter from the north gate for the first koinobori lane. Families with strollers should use the green path behind the workshop tents. The south lane is calm until album hour.

Restrooms sit between the food court and cedar tent. Water refill tables are placed at both ends of the river walk.

Guide

Small decisions made before arrival.

This is the practical layer: what to bring, where to stand, how the food windows work, and which moments are best for mixed-age families.

Bring

Hands free

A small cloth, water bottle, and one bag that can sit under a bench during the wish-card hour.

Stand

Blue lane

Best view of the streamers is the open strip between the north poles and the first food tent.

Eat

Mochi window

Preorder pickup stays on the quiet side of the counter so the tasting line can keep moving.

Make

Iris bundle

The green tent hosts short rounds. Families can join without committing to the full workshop.

Gather

Portrait hour

Meet at the coral bench after lunch when the streamer shadows cross the south lane.

Rest

Grass bank

The grass bank is kept free of vendor tables. It is the place to sit, look up, and let the day slow down.
