Visitor guide

Everything you need for a smooth day at Nobori Festival.

Getting here

Tokyo Metro Hanzomon Line to Honjo-Azumabashi Station. Exit 3, walk 6 minutes south along the river. Bicycle parking at the north gate.

What to bring

Sun hat, water bottle, picnic blanket. Cash for food stalls — no card terminals in the meadow zone. Umbrella optional; light rain will not cancel events.

Accessibility

Paved paths from north gate to ceremony stage. Wheelchair viewing area at the front of the meadow. Accessible toilets at the kitchen tent and iris garden.

Family tips

Lost-child station at the information kiosk. Stroller-friendly routes marked on the map. Nursing room inside the craft tent.
