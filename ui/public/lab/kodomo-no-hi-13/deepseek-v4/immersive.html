The avenue of carp

Each windsock carries a family's hopes into the early-summer sky. The largest, the black magoi, leads the way.

The river's bend

Like the carp that swims against the current, the journey demands strength. Every stream leads to the falls.

The waterfall

Legend says the carp that climbs the Dragon Gate waterfall transforms. Courage becomes greatness.
