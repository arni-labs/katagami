The carp that became a dragon

Kodomo no Hi isn't just Children's Day. The koinobori tell the legend of a carp that swam upstream, climbed a waterfall, and transformed into a dragon — a wish for every child's courage and growth.

Our name carries that story. Every festival we surface is a chance to see something transform.
