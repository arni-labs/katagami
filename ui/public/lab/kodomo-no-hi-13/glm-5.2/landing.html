Growth tracking

Percentile curves that actually make sense.

Enter a measurement; Koinobori plots it against WHO and Japan Paediatric Society datasets, flags outliers, and shows the trend line — not just the point.

  • Dual-standard percentiles (WHO + JPS) side by side
  • Automatic outlier detection on data entry
  • Predictive trend lines — see the trajectory, not just today
Memory timeline

A river of moments, not a folder of photos.

Every milestone, photo, and note flows in chronological order. Filter by year, by child, by category. Export the whole river as a printed photo book at any milestone — birthday, school entrance, graduation.

  • Smart auto-tagging from photo metadata and notes
  • One-tap photo book export — real print, shipped home
  • Shared with grandparents — view-only, no account needed
Milestone journal

First steps deserve more than a camera roll.

Log a milestone in seconds — photo, date, note, developmental window. Koinobori links it to the relevant growth stage and surfaces it in the timeline automatically.

  • 120+ developmental milestone templates, birth to age 18
  • Voice-to-note — dictate the moment hands-free
  • Gentle reminders aligned to developmental windows
