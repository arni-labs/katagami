こどもの日

May 5th — Children's Day. A festival of courage, growth, and the carp that climbs the waterfall to become a dragon.

An avenue of carp

Each streamer represents a child in the family. Black for the father, red for the mother, and colored streamers for each child — rising together toward the sky.

The river bends

Through green valleys and past rice fields, the water carries the spirit of early summer. Courage flows with every current.

The waterfall

The dragon gate. The carp leaps — scales catching morning light — and transforms. This is the moment every child knows: that effort becomes something more.

Become the dragon

Every child carries a dragon inside. Taketori helps families honor that journey, year after year.
