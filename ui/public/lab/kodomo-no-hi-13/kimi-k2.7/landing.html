The legend

A carp climbs the waterfall and becomes a dragon.

Kodomo no Hi is built on that promise: courage, growth, and the belief that small beings can rise. Nobori gives every family the tools to plan, share, and remember a festival weekend that feels exactly like that.

  • Plan upward: build a weekend itinerary from local events.
  • Gather kin: invite grandparents, cousins, and neighbors.
  • Send beautiful invitations that match the season.
