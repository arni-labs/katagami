Kodomo no Hi

こどもの日
鯉のぼり

The avenue begins. Tall poles hold the wishes of every child who will rise.

The river

Every carp swims
toward the fall.

Follow the bend. The water remembers the ones who made it.

The climb

Against the current
until the body changes.

You can already hear the water breaking on rock.

The waterfall • 滝

This is where
the dragon waits.

May 5. The fifth day. The day the carp learns it was always meant to fly.

龍

The dragon rises.

One child watched the whole way. Another will watch next year.
