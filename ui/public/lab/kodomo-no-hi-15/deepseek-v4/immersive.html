May 5
こどもの日

Children's Day

A celebration of courage and growth. Families raise koinobori — carp streamers — to wish strength and success for their children.

Tradition
鯉のぼり

The Carp Avenue

Each carp represents a family member. The black magoi is the father, the red higoi the mother, and smaller carp for each child — swimming upstream together.

Journey
川の道

The River Path

Like the carp that swims against the current, children grow through challenge. The river winds toward the great falls — the final test.

Courage
滝登り

The Waterfall

Legend says a carp that climbs the Dragon Gate waterfall transforms into a dragon. This is the heart of Kodomo no Hi — the belief that every child can soar.

Transformation
龍となる

The Dragon Rises

Having climbed the falls, the carp becomes a dragon — courage rewarded, growth realized. This is the wish we make for every child.
