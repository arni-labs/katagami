こどもの日

Avenue of rising carp

Begin the journey where every streamer is a wish, lifting into the clear early-summer sky.

鯉のぼり

Carried by the wind

Bright cloth ripples in the breeze. Each koinobori follows the same current, but rises in its own time.

The river bends

Green banks give way to open water. Courage is not the absence of fear — it is the decision to keep swimming.

Against the falls

The climb is steep, the current pushes back, and the summit is where the carp proves its heart.

竜

Become the dragon

At the top of the waterfall, the streamer is no longer a carp. It is legend, rising.
