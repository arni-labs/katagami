The legend

One carp. One waterfall. One gate.

An old story says a carp that swims up the waterfall and passes through the Dragon Gate becomes a dragon. Kodomo no Hi hangs koinobori to wish a child that kind of courage. Ryūmon turns the wish into a practice — a journal that climbs.
