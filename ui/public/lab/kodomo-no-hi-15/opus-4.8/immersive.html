こどもの日 May 5 · Children's Day

The valley wakes.

Dawn comes up the river. A thousand koinobori catch the first wind and lift — the whole avenue turns toward the light at once.

鯉のぼり The avenue of carp

One streamer
for each year.

Black carp for the father, red for the mother, a new bright carp for every child — added one spring at a time until the pole runs out of sky.

川 Follow the water

The river bends
toward the falls.

Every carp is swimming the same way — upstream, against the current, toward the white roar at the head of the valley.

滝 The Dragon Gate

Climb the white water.

The legend says the carp that fights its way to the top of the falls is changed. Courage carries it up the last impossible stretch.

登竜門

It becomes a dragon.

Raise a koinobori this fifth of May. Every pole says the same wish over a child: keep climbing — all the way up.
