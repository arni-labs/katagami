The old story, made daily

A carp that climbs the waterfall becomes a dragon. Your children are already climbing.

The legend behind koinobori is about courage and growth. Nobori keeps that story close — three simple parts, one for each child, every day of the year.

A streamer each

Every child gets their own carp on the family pole — their colour, their name, their river. Add a new streamer the day they arrive.

Milestones rise

Log a first word, a lost tooth, a brave climb. Each one fills the next scale on their ascent — a living record that grows with them.

Reach the Gate

Set a year's goals together. When the streamers reach the Dragon Gate, the whole family celebrates the climb — on Kodomo no Hi and every day.

For the whole family

Raise the pole together, the way you always have.

Kodomo no Hi is a morning of fresh air, green leaves and streamers snapping in the wind. Nobori keeps that feeling on your phone — calm, bright, and shared by everyone who loves your kids.
