鯉のぼり Koinobori — carp that swim in the sky

An avenue of carp

Black for the father, red for the mother, and a bright streamer for each child — 縹 blue, gold, leaf-green. The wind fills their mouths and they swim against the sky.

Upstream

The legend is old. A single carp swims against the current, up the river, where no other fish will go.

The Dragon Gate

龍門 — at the head of the river, a waterfall. The carp gathers its courage and begins to climb.

…and becomes a dragon

That is the wish behind Kodomo no Hi: that every child grows strong, brave, and true — climbing their own river, one milestone at a time.
