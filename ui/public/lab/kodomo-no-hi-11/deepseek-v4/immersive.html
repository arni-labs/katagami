こどもの日

The sky fills with colour every May

For over a thousand years, families across Japan have raised koinobori — carp streamers that dance in the spring wind, each one a wish for a child's strength and happiness.

鯉のぼり

Each carp tells a family's story

The black magoi is the father, the vermillion higoi the mother. Each smaller carp below is a child — the streamers grow with the family, year after year.

滝登り

The carp that climbs becomes a dragon

An ancient legend tells of a carp that swam upstream against the Yellow River's current and leapt the Dragon Gate waterfall — transforming into a dragon. Courage changes everything.

龍

Every child carries a dragon within

Kodomo no Hi is not just a festival. It is a promise — that every child, given love and wind, can climb any waterfall. Fly something that matters this May.
