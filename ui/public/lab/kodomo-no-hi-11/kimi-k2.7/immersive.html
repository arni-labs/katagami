Fly with the carp. 鯉のぼり

A single morning breeze carries you up the avenue of streamers, along the river, and toward the falls where courage turns into a dragon.

The avenue

Every streamer is a wish.

Black for the father, red for the mother, blue for the child — then more colours as the family grows. The poles line the path like sentinels of hope.

The river

Swim upstream.

The water quickens. The carp must push harder. Growth has never been downstream.

The falls

Courage becomes 龍.

At the thundering falls, the carp that does not turn back is transformed. The dragon rises into the last light of the day.
