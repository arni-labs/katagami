Kodomo no Hi — 5 May

Raise a streamer. Raise a dragon. こどもの日

Nobori ships everything your family needs to celebrate Children's Day — hand-finished koinobori, iris sets, and a digital story that turns your child into the carp that swam upstream.

A festival built for small hands and big hearts.

Every spring, families across Japan raise koinobori so children grow as strong and determined as the carp that becomes a dragon. Nobori brings that ritual home — with modern materials, clear instructions, and a story your child steps into.

We partner with paper artisans in Saitama and dye workshops in Tokushima to make each streamer light enough for a breeze yet vivid enough to last generations.
