Festival operating rhythm

One morning, four clean passes.

The Windscale flow follows the way Kodomo no Hi is actually run: prepare the streamers, call the wind, guide families, and close the route with care.

1

Gather the carp

Invite families, collect dedications, confirm display materials, and group streamers by neighborhood.

2

Read the wind

Set weather thresholds, assign hold calls, and prepare alternate indoor moments without scrambling.

3

Lift the route

Dispatch volunteers by segment with notes for ladders, ties, street access, and family arrivals.

4

Carry it home

Track takedown, keepsake pickup, photo sharing, and next year's earliest commitments.
