What Aozora does

Three currents that lift growth

Every feature in Aozora is designed around a single belief: growth is visible, measurable, and worth celebrating.

Milestone tracking

Define growth goals with custom milestones. Track progress visually with timeline views that show how far you've come and what's rising next.

Celebration engine

Every completed milestone triggers a celebration — badges, shared announcements, and visual koinobori that rise on your dashboard. Growth deserves recognition.

Shared journeys

Invite family members, teachers, or teammates to follow along. Comment on progress, send encouragement, and watch the streamers rise together.

The wind beneath

Set goals that flow, not stall

Aozora's goal builder turns vague aspirations into concrete, trackable milestones. Each goal becomes a current — a visual stream of progress that moves forward every day.

  • Custom milestone templates for any growth domain — academic, athletic, personal, professional
  • Automatic progress tracking with daily, weekly, and monthly cadence options
  • Visual timeline that shows the full arc of a journey, not just the current state
The celebration

Watch the koi become a dragon

In Japanese folklore, the carp that swims upstream becomes a dragon. Aozora brings that transformation to life: every milestone you complete raises a new streamer on your dashboard, and every goal you finish unlocks a golden koi — your personal dragon mark.

  • Visual koinobori that rise on your dashboard with each completed milestone
  • Golden dragon badges for completed goals — collect them across all your journeys
  • Share celebrations with your circle — family, classmates, or colleagues see the streamers rise in real time
