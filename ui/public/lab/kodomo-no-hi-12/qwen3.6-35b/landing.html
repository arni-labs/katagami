Most milestone apps feel like data entry. Kodomo no Hi feels like looking at a photo album your child wrote themselves.

Flow records, not forms

Log a moment in three taps. Snap a photo, add a line of text, pick how it feels — and the story grows organically. No fields to fill, no categories to wrestle with.

  • Voice-to-entry for quick captures on the go
  • Auto-timeline that arranges entries by age
  • Privacy-first: end-to-end encrypted

The whole village, one stream

Grandparents in Osaka, cousins in Fukuoka — everyone who loves this child can contribute. Shared family streams keep every story, every photo, every "first" in one place.

  • Role-based access: contributors, viewers, admins
  • Gentle reminders: "Has anyone seen Leo's first step?"
  • Multi-child families: one app, many stories
