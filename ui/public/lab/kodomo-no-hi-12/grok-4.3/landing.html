THE RITUAL

Four movements.
One upward year.

Koinobori turns everyday moments into a continuous story of strength and joy — just like the banners that climb higher each spring.

01
Name your carp
Give each child their own koinobori. Choose colors that tell their story. The first banner is always the most important.
02
Log the swim
Every small victory — a new word, a brave choice, a finished drawing — becomes height on the pole. The wind carries it.
03
Fly together
On Kodomo no Hi and throughout the year, gather the family, print the banners, hang them, and watch the stream rise in real time.
04
Pass the wind
Each year the pole grows taller. Your children see the record of their own courage. The oldest banners become family heirlooms.
