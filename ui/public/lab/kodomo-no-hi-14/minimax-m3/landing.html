02 — The why

Built for parents who want their children to grow up inside the year.

The festivals are not nostalgia. They are not performance. They are the rhythm a child grows inside — the May sky full of carp, the March dolls, the November kimono. NOBORI is a tool for families who want that rhythm to be present, not performed.

We don't gamify. We don't streak. We don't notify you that you haven't opened the app in 9 days. NOBORI is a calendar that respects your attention and a craft guide that respects your hands.
