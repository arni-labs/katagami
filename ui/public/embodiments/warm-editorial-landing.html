Volume One — On Craft

Reading is an act of warmth.

An interface should feel like paper that remembers the reader's hands.
❧

Type is the closest thing the web has to a voice. A serif at a comfortable measure, set on a warm ground, slows the eye just enough to let meaning arrive — and that small generosity is the whole of good editorial design.

"Comfort is not the absence of style. It is the highest form of it."

We compose for the long read: a single measured column near sixty-six characters, rules and ornaments to mark the turns, and one warm accent reserved for the moment that matters.
