Up betimes, and by the early coach down to the coast to see this famous logbook that all the town is talking of, the old keeper being three weeks dead and his books newly come to light. Paid 1s. for my place in the coach, and 6d. more to a boy for holding the horse, which vexed me. To the parsonage, where a great crowd, and the volumes laid open upon a long table, and I could see plainly how every day is set down twice: first the bare fact, the wind and the ships and the oil spent; and then again below, the very same day dressed up most finely, the sea called a great grey country and the fog a visitor that would not take its leave. Lord, to think of the old man alone in his tower these forty years, telling the plain truth to one column and lying most sweetly to the next for his own comfort, and I know not which I love him the better for. Dined at the inn upon a poor tough mutton, and paid too dear for it. Much talk whether the fine entries should be printed, the vicar all for it, the widow against, she saying her husband meant them for no eyes but his own, which I hold the honester view, though the printing would bring in good money. Home late, my head full of the thing, and so to bed.
